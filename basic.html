Here some basic skincare for the begginer!

MORNING

CLEANSER

Cleansing stimulates circulation and leaves your face feeling refreshed, which is especially helpful in the morning

MOISTURIZER

Moisturizers replenish and lock in moisture, preventing dryness caused by environmental factors like wind, cold, or indoor heating

SERUM

Vitamin C serums can help fade dark spots, even out skin tone, and give your complexion a healthy glow

SUNSCREEN

Sunscreen shields your skin from UVA rays (which cause aging and wrinkles) and UVB rays (which cause sunburn)

NIGHT

DOUBLE CLEANSER

By thoroughly removing impurities and makeup, double cleansing can help reduce the likelihood of clogged pores and acne

TONER

Toner acts as a preparatory step, helping other products like serums, treatments, and moisturizers to absorb better and work more effectively

SERUM

Night serums often contain humectants like hyaluronic acid or glycerin, which attract and lock moisture into the skin overnight, preventing dehydration

MOISTURIZER

Night moisturizers are typically more emollient and hydrating than daytime formulas. They lock in moisture, preventing your skin from becoming dry overnight
