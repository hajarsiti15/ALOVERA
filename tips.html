Here are some tips for suitable skin!

SELFCARE

CLENASER FOR OILY SKIN

Cleansers that effectively control excess sebum without stripping the skin's natural moisture barrier are ideal

SERUM FOR OILY SKIN

Use lightweight, oil-free serums to avoid clogging pores

TONER FOR OILY SKIN

Choose toners with salicylic acid, niacinamide, or tea tree oil to target oil and acne

MOISTURIZER FOR OILY SKIN

Even oily skin needs hydration, so look for oil-free or gel-based formulas that hydrate without causing breakouts

SUNSCREEN FOR OILY SKIN

Look for oil-free, matte, or gel-based sunscreens to prevent excess shine and keep pores from getting clogged
